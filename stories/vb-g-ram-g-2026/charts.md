# Chart Specifications — VB-G RAM G / MGNREGA Replacement

---

## Chart 1: MGNREGA vs VB-G RAM G — Side by Side

**Type**: Comparison table (styled as 2-column card layout)
**Purpose**: Show the 7 key differences at a glance
**Data**:

| Parameter | MGNREGA (2005-2026) | VB-G RAM G (2026-) |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Guaranteed days | 100 | 125 |
| Wage floor | None | ₹300/day |
| National avg wage | ₹298.8 | ₹327.4 |
| Centre wage share | 100% | 60% |
| State wage share | 0% | 40% |
| Funding model | Open-ended | Normative allocation |
| Seasonal pause | None | 60-day agri window |

**Colors**: Left column (MGNREGA) — muted red tones. Right column (VB-G RAM G) — gold accents.

## Chart 8: Timeline — MGNREGA to VB-G RAM G

**Type**: Vertical timeline
**Events**:
- 2005: MGNREGA passed unanimously by Parliament
- 2006: Scheme launched in 200 districts
- 2008: Extended to all 625 rural districts
- 2013: DBT introduced for wage payments
- 2016: Geo-tagging of assets mandated
- 2020: COVID surge — 7.6 Cr households employed
- 2023: e-KYC made mandatory for job cards
- Dec 2025: VB-G RAM G Act passed by Parliament
- Dec 21, 2025: Presidential assent
- May 11, 2026: Centre notifies July 1 commencement
- June 30, 2026: Wage rates notified; ₹300 floor set
- **July 1, 2026: VB-G RAM G comes into force; MGNREGA repealed**

**Colors**: Green (expansion), Red (restriction/hiatus), Gold (transition)
