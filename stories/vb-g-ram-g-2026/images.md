# Visual Intelligence Spec — VB-G RAM G / MGNREGA Replacement

---

## Hero Image

- **Subject**: Rural workers (men and women) at a worksite — road building or water harvesting — with measurement and supervision visible. One worker holds a job card or smartphone showing biometric attendance.
- **Mood**: Transition — the old and new coexisting. Dignity of labour. Bureaucratic formality in a rural setting.
- **Lighting**: Golden hour — late afternoon sun casting long shadows. Symbolic of an era ending.
- **Color palette**: Earth tones (brown, ochre, dust) with warm gold light. Muted sky.
- **Composition**: Wide shot showing the scale of the worksite. Workers in foreground, rural landscape stretching behind. A government board (old MGNREGA sign) partially visible, slightly out of focus — symbolising the past.
- **Tone**: Documentary. Not staged. Not dramatic.
- **Symbolism**: The job card / smartphone represents the transition from paper-based MGNREGA to smart-card VB-G RAM G. The physical labour is unchanged — the system around it is.

*Alternative: A close-up of weathered hands holding a new-format smart job card against the backdrop of a rural worksite.*

## Infographic 2: State Wage Map of India

**Type**: Choropleth map of India
**Purpose**: Show which states are above / at the ₹300 floor
**Color coding**:
- Dark green: Above ₹400 (Sikkim, Haryana, Goa, Kerala)
- Light green: ₹350-400 (Karnataka, Punjab)
- Amber: ₹300-350 (TN, Maharashtra, AP, Telangana)
- Grey at ₹300: All states brought up to floor (21 states)
**Callout**: "21 states raised to ₹300 floor — largest increase: Bihar (+43%)"

## Infographic 3: The Funding Shift

**Type**: Two pie charts side by side
**Left**: MGNREGA — One solid circle (Centre 100%) in blue
**Right**: VB-G RAM G — Blue 60% + Saffron 40% split
**Caption**: "Under MGNREGA, states paid 0% of wages. Under VB-G RAM G, states pay 40%."
**Variant**: Small callout for special category states (90:10)

## Data Card: 5 Key Numbers

**Type**: Stats card grid (2 rows × 3 columns on desktop, 3×2 on mobile)
**Cards**:
1. **125** — Guaranteed days per household (was 100)
2. **₹300** — Minimum daily wage (new wage floor)
3. **₹327.4** — National average daily wage (↑9.5%)
4. **40%** — State share of wage costs (was 0%)
5. **₹95,692 Cr** — FY27 Budget allocation (highest ever)
6. **21** — States brought up to wage floor
