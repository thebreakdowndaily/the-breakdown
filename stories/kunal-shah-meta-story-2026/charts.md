## Chart 7 — Kunal Shah's Delta 4 Framework
**Type:** Visual diagram (described for SVG generation)
**Alt text:** Diagram showing Shah's Delta 4 framework: products achieving a 4+ point improvement on a 10-point efficiency scale create behaviour change and irrational loyalty. Most startups achieve only Delta 1-2.
**Description:** X-axis = "Efficiency Score (1-10)", Y-axis = "User Retention". Three curves shown for Delta 1 (low retention), Delta 2 (moderate retention), Delta 4+ (near-100% retention after adoption).

## Chart 8 — The Lesson: Metrics That Matter

| Investors | Revenue growth | Capital efficiency, MoIC |
| Regulators | Compliance | Data sovereignty, market concentration |
