## Chart 4: Pre-CM vs Post-CM Comparison (Grouped Bar)
- **Type**: Grouped bar chart
- **Data**: Land holdings before and after becoming CM
  - Pre-CM (2013-2023): 179 acres across 108 plots
  - Post-CM (Dec 2023-Dec 2025): 168 acres across 137 plots
  - Rate: 85 acres in 3 years as Minister → 168 acres in 2 years as CM
- **Source**: Indian Express investigation
- **Alt text**: "Comparison showing land acquisition rate doubled after Mohan Yadav became Chief Minister"
