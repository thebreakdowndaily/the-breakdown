# Charts Specification — E20 Supreme Court Story

## Chart 1: Ethanol Blending Trajectory
- **Type**: Combined bar + line
- **X-axis**: Years (2013-14 to 2029-30)
- **Y-axis left (bars)**: Ethanol supplied (Cr L)
- **Y-axis right (line)**: Blending percentage
- **Data**: From the table in Section 3
- **Annotations**: Policy milestones (target announcements, achievement dates)

## Chart 2: Ethanol Allocation for ESY 2025-26
- **Type**: Waterfall or stacked bar
- **Components**: Total bids (1,759 Cr L) → Allocated (1,050 Cr L) → Supplied to date (680 Cr L)
- **Purpose**: Show the gap between capacity, allocation, and actual supply

## Chart 3: The Vinp Gap
- **Type**: Horizontal bar
- **Bars**: Installed capacity (9.90 Cr L) | Bid quantity (9.26 Cr L) | Initial allocation (1.44 Cr L) | Enhanced allocation (3.92 Cr L)
- **Purpose**: Visualise the discrepancy that led to the lawsuit

## Chart 4: Crude Import Savings
- **Type**: Area chart
- **X-axis**: Years
- **Y-axis**: ₹ Cr saved annually
- **Overlay**: Blending percentage as a line
- **Purpose**: Show correlation between blending rate and import savings
