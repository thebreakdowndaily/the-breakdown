## Infographic 1: What Changes / What Stays (Comparison Panel)
**Type**: Split-panel comparison graphic
**Left panel**: "MGNREGA (2005-2026)" — key stats and features
**Right panel**: "VB-G RAM G (2026 onwards)" — corresponding changes
**Colour scheme**: Left in muted grey/blue (past), right in gold (#D4A843, current)
**Key comparison items**:
- Workdays: 100 → 125
- Average wage: ₹298.8 → ₹327.4
- Wage floor: None → ₹300
- Funding: 90:10 → 60:40 (most states)
- Allocation: Demand-driven → Normative allocation
- Agricultural season: Year-round → 60-day blackout allowed
- Attendance: Manual muster → Face authentication
- Job cards: MGNREGA card → Gramin Rozgar Guarantee Card
- Legal basis: Right-based → Scheme-based with normative allocation

## Timeline Graphic: 20 Years of India's Rural Employment Guarantee
**Type**: Horizontal timeline with milestone markers
**2005**: MGNREGA passed by Parliament (Sep 7)
**2006**: Rollout begins in 200 backward districts (Feb)
**2008**: Nationwide coverage (all rural districts)
**2009**: Renamed to MGNREGA
**2020**: COVID-19 peak — 389 crore person-days
**2025**: VB-G RAM G Act passed (Dec 19), Presidential assent (Dec 21)
**2026**: VB-G RAM G comes into force (July 1)
