## Chart 5: Cost-Sharing Comparison — MGNREGA vs VB-G RAM G
**Type**: Side-by-side pie chart or stacked bar

**MGNREGA**: Centre 90%, States 10%
**VB-G RAM G (most states)**: Centre 60%, States 40%

**For NE/Himalayan states**: Centre 90%, States 10% (unchanged)
**For UTs without legislature**: Centre 100% (unchanged)

**Source**: VB-G RAM G Act, 2025, Section 12

## Chart 6: Average Days Per Household (2008-09 to 2024-25)
**Type**: Simple line or bar

Typical range: 45-55 days per household per year
Notable: Far below both 100-day (MGNREGA) and 125-day (VB-G RAM G) entitlements

**Key insight**: The gap between legal guarantee and actual delivery has persisted for 20 years. Whether the new framework closes this gap depends on implementation, not legislation.

**Source**: Drèze & Rahul R, Levy Institute WP 1095
