## STEP 3 — Hero Visual

### Concept: Temperature Anomaly Heat Map of Europe

**File name:** `europe-heatwave-2026-hero.jpg`

**Description:**
A satellite-data-informed heat map of Europe showing temperature anomalies for the period June 22-28, 2026. The continent is rendered in deep blues (normal temperatures) transitioning through yellows and oranges into deep, almost-blood reds (+10°C to +14°C above seasonal average). Seven pulsing markers indicate the locations of all-time national records. The heat dome is visible as a concentrated bullseye of extreme anomaly stretching from southwest France through central Europe toward Poland and the Baltic.

**Subject:** The European continent with extreme temperature anomaly overlay
**Mood:** Urgent, alarming, scientific — conveys a continent under thermal stress
**Lighting:** Based on Copernicus ERA5 satellite reanalysis data — emissive temperature glow effect radiating from the continent
**Color palette:**
- Background ocean/space: #070708 (editorial dark)
- Normal temps: #1a365d (deep blue) → #2b6cb0 (mid blue)
- Moderate anomaly: #ecc94b (yellow) → #ed8936 (orange)
- High anomaly: #e53e3e (red) → #742a2a (deep crimson) → #4a0000 (near-black burnt red)
- Record markers: #D4A843 (gold accent) with pulsing rings
- Text: #ffffff and #D4A843

**Composition:**
- Europe centered in frame (Mollweide or Robinson projection)
- Temperature anomaly as a continuous raster overlay (semi-transparent, emissive glow)
- Latitude/longitude grid lines in #222222 (subtle)
- 7 gold markers at: Pissos (FR), Gosport/Hampshire (UK), Coschen (DE), Słubice (PL), Doksany (CZ), Szécsény (HU), Turňa nad Bodvou (SK), plus Denmark (unlocated — marker in central Jutland)
- Temperature scale legend in bottom-right corner

**Editorial symbolism:**
The continent is literally "cooking" — the red anomaly zone appears almost like a fever scan. The gold markers suggest records falling like dominoes. The transition from warm blues in the north/west to deep reds in the center-east shows the heat dome's trajectory.

**Typography placement:**
- Top title area (safe zone): "EUROPE'S HOTTEST JUNE" in Cormorant Garamond, letter-spaced, gold
- Subtitle area: "7 All-Time Records · 1,300+ Deaths · A Continent Unprepared"

- Bottom-right: THE BREAKDOWN logo (white, small)

**Safe text area:** 80% central bounding box, 10% padding from each edge

**Aspect ratios:**
- 16:9 (primary — OG standard): 1920×1080px
- 4:3 (social/print): 1200×900px
- 1:1 (Instagram/thumbnail): 1080×1080px
- 9:16 (vertical/phone hero): 1080×1920px

### SVG Generation Standards
- viewBox must be used for all SVGs (responsive)
- All text in SVGs must be actual `<text>` elements (not images)
- Don't rely solely on color — use patterns (dots, stripes, hatches) for chart fills
- Minimum color contrast ratio: 4.5:1 (all text/background combinations)
- Grid lines: opacity 0.15-0.3, stroke #222222
- Gold borders: stroke #D4A843, 1-2px
- Round corners: rx="4" for cards, rx="2" for interactive elements

### Accessibility Requirements
- Every SVG: `<title>` and `<desc>` tags with meaningful text
- Every image: `alt` attribute with full description
- Charts: Include data labels directly on the chart, not just in legend
- Responsive: viewBox scales to container width
- Color contrast: Test all text/background combos against WCAG AA (4.5:1)
- Focus indicators: Visible focus states for any interactive elements
