## Chart 7: Penalty Enforcement Comparison

**Type**: Simple comparison card / icon array
**Purpose**: Show the absurdity of ₹453 in penalties

**Panel A — What the law allows**:
- Maximum penalty per case: ₹25,000
- Number of rejection cases: 58,501
- Theoretical maximum: ₹1.46 crore

**Panel B — What was collected**:
- Total penalties collected: ₹453
- This is: 0.003% of maximum
- This is: Less than 50 RTI application fees
- Breakdown: MEA ₹2, DoPT ₹79, Coal ₹17, Delhi ₹4, MSME ₹1

**Comparison icons**:
- ₹453 = Cost of 2 mid-range restaurant meals in Delhi
- ₹1.46 Cr = Cost of 1,00,000+ meals

**Color**: Dark red background for Panel A, gold for comparison
