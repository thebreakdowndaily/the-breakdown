1.8 TMC Rebel MPs Merged with NCPI (June 14, 2026)

  • Status: CONFIRMED. 20 TMC Lok Sabha MPs, led by Kakoli Ghosh Dastidar and Sudip Bandyopadhyay, merged with Nationalist Citizens Party of India (NCPI) and declared support for NDA.
  • Sources: The News Mill (June 14), Deccan Herald (June 14), Hindustan Times (June 15), Deccan Chronicle (June 15), Times of India (June 16).
  • NCPI details: Registered as a political party in January 2023, headquartered in Sankarail, Howrah district, West Bengal. Fielded 4 candidates in Tripura assembly polls 2023 — max 536 votes. Previously obscure party that overnight shot to national limelight.
  • Leadership change: Kakoli Ghosh Dastidar was elected NCPI president on May 30, 2026, after incumbent Shewly Kundu resigned. The rebel MPs effectively took over the party before announcing the merger. Source: Times of India (June 16).
  • Legal status: The group claims 2/3rd majority of TMC's Lok Sabha strength (20 of 28 MPs). Under anti-defection law, a merger with another party by 2/3rds of members may avoid disqualification. This is being challenged by TMC loyalist MPs Kirti Azad and Sagarika Ghose, who filed a complaint with Lok Sabha Speaker.
  • July 2026 plan: The rebel faction intends to formally claim the "Trinamool Congress" name in July through legal process, per Sudip Bandyopadhyay. Source: The News Mill (June 14).
  • BJP connection: West Bengal CM Suvendu Adhikari, Union Minister Bhupender Yadav and MP Nishikant Dubey have been involved in discussions with the rebels. Several meetings occurred at Yadav's residence.
  • Confidence: HIGH (event confirmed). MEDIUM (legal status of merger — being challenged in Speaker's court).

1.9 Operation Tiger — Six Shiv Sena (UBT) MPs Defected (June 17-22, 2026)

  • Status: CONFIRMED and COMPLETE. Six of nine Shiv Sena (UBT) Lok Sabha MPs formally joined Eknath Shinde-led Shiv Sena on June 22, 2026.
  • Shinde's declaration: "Operation Tiger is now complete and successful... My operations are foolproof." He described the defecting MPs as "Dhurandhar" (skilled) leaders with strong grassroots connections. Source: National Herald (June 22).
  • CM Fadnavis confirmation: "The operation has been successful, and the body is in very good health." Source: Rediff (June 22).
  • COMPLETE MP LIST (all 6 confirmed names):
    • Sanjay Deshmukh — Yavatmal (Ramtek) constituency
    • Sanjay Jadhav — Parbhani constituency
    • Sanjay Dina Patil — Mumbai North East constituency
    • Nagesh Patil-Ashtikar — Hingoli constituency
    • Omprakash Rajenimbalkar — Dharashiv (Osmanabad) constituency
    • Bhausaheb Wakchaure — Shirdi constituency
  • Timeline: June 16-17 — Six MPs skipped Sena (UBT) parliamentary party meeting in Delhi. June 17 — Eknath Shinde met them in Delhi. June 18-21 — Legal and political negotiations. June 22 — Formal induction at Shinde's event.
  • Political significance: Sena (UBT) reduced from 9 to 3 Lok Sabha MPs. Shinde Sena became 2nd largest party in Maharashtra in Lok Sabha representation. All 6 MPs promised to contest 2029 on Shiv Sena symbol.
  • Sources: The Week (June 17), Times of India (June 22), Indian Express (June 13, 17), IBTimes (June 17), National Herald (June 22), Rediff (June 22), New Indian Express (June 16).
  • Confidence: HIGH
