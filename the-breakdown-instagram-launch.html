Step 1

Account Setup

1

Create the Account

Switch to a Creator Account. Get better analytics, smaller category label, easier contact options.

  • Username: @thebreakdown (backup: @thebreakdownhq)
  • Category: News & Media or Journalist
  • Contact info: add email for business inquiries
2

Profile Picture

Use the B icon mark from the logo pack. Gold on charcoal circle. 320x320px minimum.

  • Export from the-breakdown-logo-pack.html
  • Use "Icon Mark (Light)" variant
  • Test at small size - must be recognizable as a B
3

Bio

Keep it clean. One line of what you do. One line of what you cover. One CTA.

  • Line 1: Complex stories. Clear analysis.
  • Line 2: Geopolitics | Tech | Economy | Media
  • Line 3: New breakdowns every Tue & Fri
  • Line 4: Full analysis below + link
4

Website Link

Use linktr.ee/thebreakdown or your custom website. Link to: YouTube, Newsletter, X, About page.

  • Primary link: YouTube latest video
  • Secondary: Newsletter signup
  • Tertiary: X account

Grid Rules

Pattern: [Carousel] [Reel/Static] [Carousel] [Carousel] [Reel/Static] [Carousel]

Color: All posts use #1a1a2e background. Dark grid = cohesive.

B mark: Bottom-right corner on every post. Small. Semi-transparent.

No photos: Graphics only. Icons, text, data. No stock photos.

The 3-Grid Rule:
Before posting, check last 9 posts:
1. Consistent color palette
2. Alternating formats
3. Visual hierarchy (featured stands out)

Step 8

Launch Checklist

Pre-Launch (1 Week)

  • Create Instagram account
  • Set up profile (bio, PFP, link)
  • Create 5 highlight covers
  • Design first 4 carousels
  • Edit first 2 Reels
  • Schedule pre-launch stories
  • Cross-promote on X and YouTube
  • Set up linktr.ee

Launch Day (Tuesday)

  • Post teaser stories (Mon 8 PM)
  • Post first carousel (Tue 12 PM)
  • Post Reel (Tue 6 PM)
  • Post YouTube promo story
  • Engage with all comments (1hr)
  • Share to Stories from others
  • Monitor analytics

Post-Launch (Week 1)

  • Post 2-3 carousels/week
  • Post 1-2 Reels/week
  • Post daily Stories
  • Engage 10 target accounts/day
  • Reply to all DMs (24hr)
  • Track metrics daily
  • Adjust strategy based on data
