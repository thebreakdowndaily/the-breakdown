DEPLOY THE BREAKDOWN

Step-by-step guide to launch thebreakdown.in

Before You Start

Make sure you have:

  • GoDaddy account with thebreakdown.in domain
  • Email access (for Netlify verification)
  • This folder: C:\newsjack-content\deploy\
STEP 1

Create Netlify Account

  1. Go to app.netlify.com
  2. Click "Sign up"
  3. Use your email (no GitHub needed)
  4. Verify your email address
Open Netlify
STEP 2

Deploy Website

  1. After login, click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
  2. Open the deploy folder on your computer
  3. Drag the entire folder into Netlify
  4. Wait for upload to complete
  5. Netlify will give you a URL like xyz123.netlify.app
  6. Copy this URL — you'll need it for DNS
STEP 3

Connect Custom Domain

  1. In Netlify dashboard → Site settings → Domain management
  2. Click "Add custom domain"
  3. Type: thebreakdown.in
  4. Click "Verify" → "Add domain"
STEP 4

GoDaddy DNS Setup

  1. Log in to godaddy.com → My Products
  2. Find thebreakdown.in → Click "DNS"
  3. Delete any existing A or CNAME records
  4. Add these two records:
Type: A     Name: @     Value: 75.2.60.5     TTL: 600
Type: CNAME     Name: www     Value: YOUR-SITE.netlify.app     TTL: 600
  1. Click Save
STEP 5

Wait & Test

DNS propagation takes 5 minutes to 48 hours (usually 1-4 hours).

  1. Wait 1 hour
  2. Visit https://thebreakdown.in
  3. Check SSL (padlock icon in browser)
  4. Test on mobile
STEP 6

Set Up Newsletter (Optional)

For email signup, use Netlify Forms:

  1. In Netlify dashboard → Forms
  2. The form on your site will auto-detect
  3. Add notification email in Form notifications
