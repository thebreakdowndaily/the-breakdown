# THE BREAKDOWN — Trending Intelligence

Find the most searched, discussed, fastest growing, and highest impact stories.

## Process

1. Search for top trends across:
   - Google Trends (India focus)
   - News headlines (Reuters, BBC, Indian Express, The Hindu)
   - Government announcements (PIB, ministries)
   - Research papers (arXiv, Google Scholar)
   - Social discussions (X/Twitter, Reddit)
   - Wikipedia current events

2. For each trend, collect:
   - Topic title
   - Search volume / velocity
   - News outlets covering it
   - Why it's trending now
   - Category (Government, Economy, Technology, Geopolitics, Education, Policy, Media)

3. Rank every story by:
   - Urgency (breaking vs evergreen)
   - Impact (how many people it affects)
   - Data availability
   - Visual potential
   - Audience interest

## Output Format

Produce a ranked list of trending stories, each with:
- Rank
- Topic
- Category
- Trending since
- Why it matters (one line)
- Score (1-100)
- Recommended action: Publish Now / Monitor / Ignore

Do NOT write articles. Only identify and rank trends.
