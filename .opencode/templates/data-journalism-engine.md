EDITORIAL PRINCIPLES

Never manipulate scales to exaggerate trends.

Always label axes clearly.

Show uncertainty when appropriate.

Separate observed data from projections.

Cite every dataset.

Distinguish official statistics from estimates.

If multiple credible sources disagree, explain the difference rather than choosing one without justification.
