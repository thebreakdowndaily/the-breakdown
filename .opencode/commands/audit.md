---

Act as a public policy auditor, governance analyst, investigative researcher, and accountability expert.

Search the web for information about $ARGUMENTS.

Audit categories: scheme, ministry, policy, politician, government.

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

- What is being audited
- Purpose / mandate
- Overall performance rating (1–10)
- Key strengths
- Key failures
- Bottom-line assessment

## 2. BACKGROUND & CONTEXT

- When was it established / launched
- Original objectives
- Budget allocated
- Target beneficiaries / stakeholders
- Legal / constitutional basis

## 3. PERFORMANCE METRICS

- Quantitative indicators
- Qualitative indicators
- Targets vs. actuals
- Timeline of milestones
- Outcome data (with sources)

## 4. FINANCIAL AUDIT

- Total funds allocated vs. released vs. spent
- Utilization percentage
- Major cost heads
- Audit findings (CAG, PAC, etc.)
- Instances of waste / diversion / misappropriation
- Value-for-money assessment

## 5. IMPLEMENTATION ASSESSMENT

- Delivery mechanism
- Ground-level execution
- Bottlenecks and delays
- Grievance redressal
- Transparency and disclosure

## 6. IMPACT ANALYSIS

- Intended vs. actual impact
- Beneficiary feedback
- Independent evaluations
- Unintended consequences
- Comparative performance (vs. peers / predecessors)

## 7. CONTROVERSIES & CRITICISM

For each controversy:

- Date / period
- Allegation
- Evidence available
- Official response
- Inquiry / investigation status
- Resolution

Clearly distinguish: Allegation, Investigation, Proof, Admission, Action Taken.

## 8. LEGAL & REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

- Statutory compliance
- Court cases / pending litigation
- Regulatory violations
- Show-cause notices
- Penalties imposed

## 9. STAKEHOLDER ANALYSIS

- Who benefits
- Who is excluded
- Who opposes
- Who profits
- Civil society / media perspectives

## 10. RECOMMENDATIONS

- Short-term fixes
- Structural reforms
- Accountability measures
- Best practice benchmarks
- Termination / continuation recommendation

## 11. TIMELINE

Chronological timeline of key events from inception to present.

## 12. SOURCES

List all sources with citations: government reports, CAG reports, court orders, news investigations, academic papers, RTI data.
