Act as an investigative accountability researcher, promise tracker, and public policy auditor.

Research: $ARGUMENTS

Create a Government Accountability Report.

Search:

* Election manifestos and party promises
* Government statements and press releases
* Parliamentary questions and debates
* CAG audit reports
* PAC committee reports
* RTI responses
* News investigations and fact-check websites
* Independent watchdog reports
* Court judgments on public interest cases

Generate:

## 1. Promises Register

For each major promise/pledge:

* Promise
* Date made
* Source (manifesto, speech, budget)
* Original timeline
* Current status (Delivered / In Progress / Delayed / Abandoned / Broken)
* Evidence of delivery or failure

## 2. Budget Delivery

* Promised spending vs actual spending
* Schemes announced vs schemes operational
* Fund utilisation rates
* Unspent balances and surrendered amounts

## 3. Project Delivery

* Major projects announced vs completed
* Timeline slippage
* Cost overruns (original vs current estimate)
* stalled projects (with reasons)

## 4. Audit Findings

* CAG performance audits
* Key irregularities flagged
* Instances of waste, fraud, misappropriation
* Audit paras still open/unresolved
* PAC recommendations pending implementation

## 5. Policy Outcomes

* Did announced policies achieve stated goals?
* Intended vs actual impact
* Independent evaluations
* Beneficiary feedback / grievance data

## 6. Transparency Score

* Is data publicly available?
* Are dashboards updated?
* Is RTI functioning?
* Are procurement details published?
* Whistleblower protections

## 7. Accountability Gaps

* Who is responsible for failure?
* Was any action taken?
* Were promises legally binding?
* Can citizens seek remedy?

## 8. Verdict

Rate on a scale of 1-10:

* Promise keeping
* Budget honesty
* Project management
* Audit responsiveness
* Transparency
* Overall accountability

Output as an evidence-based report with specific citations for each claim.
