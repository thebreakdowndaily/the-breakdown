## YOUTUBE VIDEO INTRO

### Duration: 7 seconds

**SECOND 0-1: BLACK SCREEN**
- Pure black. No sound. One beat of silence.

**SECOND 1-2: THE BREAK**
- A thin gold line (#e4a11d) draws horizontally across center screen, left to right.
- **SFX:** A clean, metallic snap — like a bone breaking, but precise. Not violent. Satisfying.
- The line splits in the middle and falls apart slightly — creating a visible gap.
- The gap is the divide. The analysis begins.

**SECOND 2-3: THE B FORMS**
- From the broken gold line, a bold uppercase "B" assembles itself.
- Left half: solid deep charcoal (#1a1a2e).
- Right half: splits into gold top segment and charcoal bottom segment.
- The B fills center screen. Dominant. Confident.

**SECOND 3-5: WORDMARK**
- The B shrinks and moves left.
- "THE" types in (small caps, tracked, light weight) to the right of B.
- "BREAKDOWN" types in below (bold condensed caps) — letters appear one by one, left to right.
- **SFX:** Each letter produces a soft mechanical click. Subtle. Rhythmic.
- A thin gold line draws under "BREAKDOWN."

**SECOND 5-6: TAGLINE**
- "Complex stories. Clear analysis." fades in below in italic serif (Georgia).
- Color: warm gray (#888).
- **SFX:** Low cinematic drone begins building — a single sustained note.

**SECOND 6-7: LOCK**
- All elements hold position for 1 second. Clean. Authoritative.
- **SFX:** The drone fades. A single clean beat.
- Hard cut to content.

## YOUTUBE VIDEO OUTRO

### Duration: 12 seconds

**SECOND 0-1: RECAP (if applicable)**
- Quick flash of the most dramatic frame from the video — 0.5 seconds.
- This reminds viewers of the value they just received.

**SECOND 1-4: RECOMMENDATION CARDS**
- Three video thumbnails appear in a grid layout (2 left, 1 right).
- Each thumbnail has a subtle gold border (2px, #e4a11d).
- Title text below each: white on charcoal (#1a1a2e).
- **Animation:** Cards slide in from bottom, one at a time (stagger 0.3s).
- **SFX:** Soft whoosh per card.

**SECOND 4-7: SUBSCRIBE PROMPT**
- Below the cards, the B icon mark (small, ~40px) appears center-left.
- "SUBSCRIBE" text in bold caps (Bebas Neue) next to it.
- A gold ring animation pulses outward from the B mark — like a radar ping — drawing attention.
- **SFX:** Single, resonant bell tone.

**SECOND 7-10: CTA TEXT**
- Cards and subscribe prompt fade.
- New text appears center-screen, stacked:

```
IF YOU UNDERSTAND THE STORY,
YOU UNDERSTAND THE WORLD.

We break it down every week.
Subscribe.
```

- Font: Source Serif Pro (first two lines) / Inter (bottom lines)
- Color: white on charcoal
- **Animation:** Text fades in line by line, stagger 0.4s.

**SECOND 10-12: END**
- All text holds for 1 second.
- B mark appears small, center-bottom, with gold accent line.
- Fade to black.
- **SFX:** Same drone from intro, one final note, then silence.
