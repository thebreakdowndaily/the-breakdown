Send a Tip

We protect our sources. If you have a document, tip, or story idea, here's how to reach us.

Secure Drop

For sensitive documents and communications, we strongly recommend encrypted channels. Create a free ProtonMail account and email us, or reach us via Signal for end-to-end encrypted messaging. We review every submission and will reach out if we need additional details.

Encrypted Email

tips@thebreakdown.in — We recommend using ProtonMail or GPG encryption for sensitive tips. Request our public PGP key via a separate channel before sending encrypted materials.

Direct Message

X (Twitter) DM

Send us a direct message on X: @thebreakdownin. For anonymous tips, we recommend creating a burner account and using Signal instead.

Privacy commitment: We use Signal for encrypted communication. We never reveal sources without explicit consent. If you request anonymity, we will protect your identity — even if we are asked to disclose it. Our editorial process is designed to minimize exposure of source identities.
