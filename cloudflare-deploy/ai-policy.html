AI Policy

How We Use AI

The Breakdown uses artificial intelligence as a tool to enhance our analysis, not replace human judgment. This page explains how.

AI-Assisted Content

Some stories on The Breakdown are generated with AI assistance. This means:

  • AI helps gather and synthesize information from multiple sources
  • AI assists in drafting initial analysis
  • Human editors review, verify, and approve all content before publication
  • AI-generated content is clearly labeled as such

AI Analysis Cards

Story pages may include "AI Analysis" cards that provide machine-generated context and connections. These are:

  • Generated by AI based on the story content
  • Reviewed by editors for accuracy
  • Labeled to distinguish from human-written analysis

What AI Does Not Do

  • AI does not make editorial decisions
  • AI does not determine what stories we cover
  • AI does not replace human fact-checking
  • AI does not write final headlines or conclusions

Transparency

We believe in transparency about AI use. If you have questions about how AI is used in a specific story, contact us at ai-policy@thebreakdown.in.

Updates

This policy is updated as our use of AI evolves. Last updated: June 2026.
