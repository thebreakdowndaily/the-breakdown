Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What We Collect

The Breakdown is designed to minimise data collection. We collect only what's necessary to operate and improve the service:

  • Newsletter signups: Your email address, stored via our email provider solely for sending the Sunday Briefing. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Analytics (Plausible): Page views, referrer, device type, and country — anonymously, no cookies, no personal data. We use Plausible because it respects your privacy by design.
  • Reading history (local only): Stories you follow are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No cookies for tracking or advertising
  • No IP address logging beyond transient connection data
  • No behavioural profiling
  • No personal identifiers beyond email for newsletter subscribers
  • No third-party tracking scripts, pixels, or embedded trackers

How We Use Your Data

Email addresses are used exclusively to send the newsletter you subscribed to. We never share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising, AI training, or any purpose beyond the specific service you requested.

Data Retention

Newsletter emails are retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained by Plausible per their privacy policy (anonymised, no personal data). Local storage data persists until you clear your browser data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time via the link in every email
  • Clear your localStorage reading history via browser settings
  • Request confirmation of what data we hold by emailing hello@thebreakdown.in

Children's Privacy

The Breakdown is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date and, for material changes, notify newsletter subscribers.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@thebreakdown.in.
