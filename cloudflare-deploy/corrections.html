Corrections

Our Commitment

The Breakdown is committed to accuracy. When we make mistakes, we correct them promptly and transparently.

How to Report an Error

If you spot an error in our reporting, please email us at corrections@thebreakdown.in with:

  • The URL of the story containing the error
  • A description of the error
  • Sources supporting the correction (if available)

Our Process

When we receive a correction request:

  • We review the claim within 24 hours
  • If the error is confirmed, we correct the story and note the correction
  • If the claim is disputed, we investigate further
  • We reply to the reporter within 48 hours
