What We Are

The Breakdown is a forensic newsroom. We track the deals, decisions, and deceptions shaping modern India — and we trace every claim to its source. No punditry. No press releases. Just the chain of evidence.

We cover geopolitics, technology, the economy, policy, media, and national security with the rigour of an investigative desk and the clarity of a briefing room. Our stories don't tell you what to think. They show you what happened, who did it, and why it matters.

Our promise: Every story on The Breakdown will still be worth reading in five years. We don't chase the news cycle. We track the forces moving the needle — and we keep tracking them until the story is resolved.

Why We Exist

Indian media is broken. The 24-hour news cycle rewards speed over accuracy. Ad-driven models reward outrage over analysis. PR wire rewrites pass as journalism. And readers are left with noise, not signal.

The Breakdown exists to be the signal. We are:

  • Reader-supported, not advertiser-driven: Our only incentive is your trust
  • Forensic, not opinionated: We trace every claim to primary sources
  • Persistent, not reactive: We follow stories until they reach resolution
  • Transparent, not opaque: We show our work, our sources, and our methods

Our Methodology

Every dispatch on The Breakdown follows a structured investigation process:

  • Primary source first: We begin with government data, official documents, court filings, parliamentary records, and first-hand reporting — not news articles about news articles.
  • Corroboration: Every factual claim is cross-verified against at least two independent sources before publication. Claims that cannot be independently verified are clearly labelled as such.
  • Evidence hierarchy: We rank sources by reliability — official data and legal documents (Tier 1), primary reporting and expert testimony (Tier 2), verified secondary sources (Tier 3), and contextual sources (Tier 4). Each story's composite confidence score is disclosed.
  • Living documents: Stories are updated as new information emerges. Updates are logged and dated. Changed information is clearly marked.

Funding & Independence

The Breakdown is entirely reader-supported. We accept:

  • Individual reader subscriptions and memberships
  • Grants from journalism foundations (with no editorial control)

We do not accept:

  • Advertising or sponsored content of any kind
  • Funding from political parties, corporations, or government bodies
  • Payment for coverage, placement, or access

This independence is written into our founding constitution. It is not negotiable.
