Funding & Independence

Last updated: June 2026

The Breakdown is an independent, reader-supported newsroom. We believe journalism that answers to readers — not advertisers, shareholders, or political patrons — produces the most trustworthy work. This page is our public accounting.

How We Are Funded

We have a deliberately simple funding model designed to preserve total editorial independence:

  • ✓ Reader subscriptions: Individual readers who value our work choose to support us via monthly or annual memberships.
  • ✓ Journalism grants: We accept grants from philanthropic foundations with clean editorial firewalls. No grant is accepted if it comes with editorial conditions.
  • ✓ Newsletter sponsorships (future): If introduced, they will be clearly labelled, from vetted partners, and firewalled from editorial decisions.

Pledge: We will never accept funding that compromises our editorial independence. If a funding source creates a conflict, we disclose it — or decline it. Every decision about what to cover, how to cover it, and what to publish is made by our editorial team alone.

What We Do Not Accept

  • ✗ No advertising: No display ads, no native ads, no sponsored content. We do not sell our readers' attention.
  • ✗ No corporate funding: We do not accept money from corporations, including for event sponsorships or research partnerships.
  • ✗ No political funding: We accept nothing from political parties, campaigns, or affiliated organizations.
  • ✗ No government funding: No grants from government bodies or state-affiliated institutions.
  • ✗ No pay-for-play: We do not charge for coverage, interviews, or placement. We do not accept payment for access.
  • ✗ No crypto or speculative funding: We do not accept donations in cryptocurrency or from entities whose funding sources cannot be transparently traced.

Financial Transparency

We commit to publishing an annual transparency report detailing our revenue sources, expenses, and any changes to our funding model. Our goal is to make trust verifiable, not just claimable.

Editorial Independence

Our editorial constitution guarantees independence. Key provisions:

  • No funder — individual, institutional, or otherwise — has editorial input or advance access to our reporting
  • No funding source is ever promised coverage, favourable or otherwise
  • All funding sources above a material threshold are publicly disclosed
  • The editorial team has final say on all content decisions

Report a Concern

If you believe any of these commitments have been compromised, or if you have questions about our funding, email hello@thebreakdown.in.
