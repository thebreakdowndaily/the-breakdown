Editorial Policy

Our Mission

The Breakdown exists to provide forensic news analysis that goes beyond headlines. We break down complex stories in geopolitics, technology, economy, and media with precision and clarity.

Standards

  • Accuracy: Every claim is verified against multiple credible sources before publication.
  • Fairness: We present multiple perspectives on contested issues.
  • Transparency: We disclose our methods, sources, and any conflicts of interest.
  • Independence: We are not affiliated with any political party, corporation, or advocacy group.

Source Verification

All stories undergo our verification process: primary source confirmation, cross-referencing with established outlets, and expert review where applicable. We tag each source as Verified, Primary, or Contextual.

AI Disclosure

Some content on The Breakdown is generated with AI assistance. All AI-generated content is clearly labeled and reviewed by human editors before publication. See our AI Policy for details.

Corrections

If you find an error, please report it here. We correct errors promptly and transparently.
