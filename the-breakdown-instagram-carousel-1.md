## VISUAL STYLE GUIDE

### Color Palette
- Background: #1a1a2e (deep charcoal) or #0f0f1a (near black)
- Text: #ffffff (white) for primary, #888888 (warm gray) for secondary
- Accent: #e4a11d (gold) for highlights, emphasis, key lines
- Danger: #dc3545 (red) for negative outcomes

### Typography
- Headlines: Bebas Neue or Impact, all caps, tracked
- Body: Source Sans Pro or Inter, regular weight
- Emphasis: Bold or gold color (not both)

### Layout
- 1080 x 1080 (square) — optimal for Instagram feed
- Safe zone: 100px from edges
- Text minimum: 24px
- B icon mark: always bottom-right corner, small, semi-transparent

### Transitions
- Slide 1→2: Fade
- Slide 2→3: Slide up
- Slide 3→4: Fade
- Slide 4→5: Slide up
- Slide 5→6: Fade
- Slide 6→7: Slide up
- Slide 7→8: Fade
- Slide 8→9: Slide up
- Slide 9→10: Fade

**Rule:** Alternate between fade and slide. Keeps it dynamic without being chaotic.
