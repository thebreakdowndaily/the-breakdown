# MEDIA CHANNEL BRAND IDENTITY KIT

## Channel Name: UNPACKED
*Tagline: How the world works — explained.*

---

## 1. BRAND STRATEGY

**Mission:** Turn complex news into clear analysis — explaining not just *what* happened, but *why* it matters and *what comes next*.

**Audience:**
- Primary: 22–40, educated professionals, curious about geopolitics/tech/policy
- Secondary: 18–22, students preparing for competitive exams (UGC NET, UPSC)
- Tertiary: 40+, informed citizens who want depth beyond headlines

**Brand Voice:**
| Dimension | Style |
|-----------|-------|
| Tone | Authoritative but accessible |
| Temperature | Warm, not cold. Human, not robotic. |
| Complexity | Explains jargon without dumbing down |
| Perspective | Balanced, presents both sides |
| Hook | Curiosity-driven, contradiction-led |

**Brand Pillars:**
1. **Clarity** — "No jargon, no spin, just signal"
2. **Context** — "Every story has a backstory. We tell both."
3. **Critical thinking** — "Don't tell me what to think. Tell me how to think about it."
4. **Consistency** — "Same quality, every time"

---

## 2. LOGO CONCEPT

### Primary Logo: "The Unfold"

**Visual:** A stylized lowercase "u" that doubles as an unfolding page — the right stem of the "u" curls outward like a page being turned, suggesting revelation/discovery. A single dot above the curve (like a full stop) in accent color.

**Color:** Deep navy (#0f172a) primary, with a gold (#f59e0b) accent dot.

**Typography:** Custom wordmark "unpacked" in bold, clean sans-serif (closely related to Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans), all lowercase. The "u" is the logo mark; the rest of the word sits beside it.

**Variants:**
- **Full logo:** Icon + "unpacked" wordmark side by side
- **Icon only:** The "u" unfold mark (for social avatars, favicon)
- **Wordmark only:** "unpacked" in brand typography (for headers, merch)

### Secondary Logo (Stacked)

For vertical spaces: icon above the wordmark, centered.

### Monochrome Variant

All-white version for dark backgrounds. All-black version for print.

## 4. VISUAL IDENTITY

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Deep Navy | `#0f172a` | Headers, backgrounds, logo |
| Secondary | Warm Gold | `#f59e0b` | Accents, highlights, CTA |
| Neutral | Off-White | `#f8fafc` | Page backgrounds |
| Text | Dark Slate | `#1e293b` | Body text |
| Accent 1 | Signal Blue | `#3b82f6` | Links, data viz |
| Accent 2 | Alert Red | `#dc2626` | Breaking news, urgency |
| Accent 3 | Balanced Green | `#10b981` | Positive trends, confirmations |

### Typography

| Role | Font | Fallback |
|------|------|----------|
| Headlines (print) | Playfair Display (serif) | Georgia |
| Headlines (digital) | Plus Jakarta Sans (sans) | Segoe UI |
| Body | Inter | Arial |
| Data / Code | JetBrains Mono | Courier New |
| Pull Quotes | Playfair Display italic | Georgia italic |

**Hierarchy:**
- Title: 48/36/28px (desktop/tablet/mobile)
- Subhead: 24/20/18px
- Body: 16/15/14px
- Caption: 12px

### Grid System

- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (primary), 1:1 (social), 4:5 (Instagram feed), 9:16 (Shorts/Reels)
- **Margins:** 80px (desktop), 40px (tablet), 20px (mobile)
- **Columns:** 12-column grid
- **White space:** Generous. Nothing feels crowded.

## 8. CHANNEL ART EXAMPLES

### YouTube Banner Description

A dark navy background with a subtle world map outline in very low opacity (10%). The "unpacked" logo in the top-left corner in white/gold. Tagline "How the world works — explained." centered below. Three feature boxes on the right showing "Geopolitics | Tech | Economy" in gold-outlined pills. Warm gold accent line running horizontally beneath the logo.

### Instagram Grid Strategy

- **Rows 1, 4, 7:** Carousel covers (bold typography, dark background, gold accents)
- **Rows 2, 5, 8:** Quote/text posts (minimal, high contrast)
- **Rows 3, 6, 9:** Infographics / data visualizations (colorful, informative)

Every 3rd post: a video/reel tile (identified by a small play icon overlay).

### X Profile

- **Avatar:** Icon-only "u" unfold mark on navy circle
- **Banner:** Dark gradient with small repeating text pattern "signal not noise" in ultra-low opacity
- **Bio:** "How the world works — explained. Geopolitics, Tech, Economy. 📩 Newsletter: [link]"
- **Pinned post:** Most popular thread or newsletter signup
