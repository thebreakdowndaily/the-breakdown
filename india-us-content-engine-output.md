**Opportunity angle:** The Indian diaspora in the US is watching. H-1B restrictions + dead sailors = double whammy for the "Indian-American success story" narrative.

### STORY 3: USTR Greer to Visit India June 23-24 — Trade Deal Final Lap

**Headline:** India-US Trade Deal Inches Closer as USTR Lands in Delhi Next Week

**Why people care:** A trade deal means lower prices, more jobs, and certainty. No deal means 12.5% additional tariffs under Section 301. Indian exporters (textiles, pharma, auto parts) are watching every signal.

**Key insight:** The July 24 deadline is real — 10% universal tariffs lapse then. If no deal by then, MFN tariffs kick in. This is the last window before November US elections freeze everything.

**Contrarian angle:** India is demanding "preferential tariff treatment and assurances against future tariff hikes." Trump doesn't do "assurances." The deal may be interim, but trust deficit is structural.

**Opportunity angle:** The forced labour narrative (Section 301) is a wildcard. India can flip this by showcasing labour reforms and compliance frameworks — turning a threat into a PR win.
